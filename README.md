## 🎯 About Me

> *"I don't just analyze data — I teach people how to speak its language."*

I'm a passionate **Data Analyst & Business Intelligence Analyst** who believes that data literacy is a superpower everyone should have. Through **Amanam Teaches**, I help professionals and beginners alike unlock the potential of data, AI, and modern tech tools.

- 🔭 Currently building data-driven solutions and BI dashboards
- 🎓 Creating educational content on **SQL, Python, Power BI, Excel & AI**
- 📝 Writing technical blogs and tutorials
- 🎥 Producing video content to make tech accessible to everyone
- 🤝 Open to collaborations on data projects, workshops, and mentorship
